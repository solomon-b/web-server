## 0.1.0.0 — 2024-12-09

Initial release. Provides QuasiQuoters for embedding XML/HTML
fragments and documents into Haskell source as `xmlhtml` `Node` and
`Document` values, used by the example server's templating layer.
See `git log` for pre-baseline history.
